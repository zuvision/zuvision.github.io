What we do


FUNDRAISING

ENGINEERING


We are builders. Onboard our space shuttle is a dedicated crew of the very best engineers across different aspects of tech. We can help you with hiring, building, scaling, refactoring, processes, systems, languages, models, prototyping and more. Engineering is hard, so do it with dedicated experts.

PRODUCT


We are product led, product obsessed. Our product folks are the very best in the industry and can ensure success on the journey to product-market-fit. We are all about users, design, usability, customers and scaling. We care about building good products for good people that ultimately realize how we function as humans.

GROWTH


The growth engine is what keeps the space shuttle forever exploring and going beyond! We are a team of expert growth & marketing folks. FINANCE


Finance is hard. From bookkeeping to accounting, specialist CPA services and tax advice, we have a dedicated team of experts who can help on this front. BUSINESS


We have in-house tried and tested, famed and trusted former CEOs. We know what it takes to fundraise, scale and succeed in business. CULTURE


Culture is everything. We have built, scaled and informed several companies on exemplary cultural practices. SYSTEMS

Let us take a look - we are systems obsessed.
